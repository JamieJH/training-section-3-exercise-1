Decision Maker!

    Edit Decision Tree

    * New Decision title should be unique, else it will replace the current Decision with the same title.

    ** New answer should be unique, should you ever want to create a decision from that answer.

    Instructions

    Action Instructions
    Add new Decision Choose a parent decision, enter the decision and its answers.
    Each answer are separated by a new line.
    Add new Answers to a Decision Choose a decision and enter new answers.
    Each answer are separated by a new line.
    Edit Decision Question Choose a decision and enter new question.
    Edit Decision Answers Choose a decision, select the old answers you want to edit, then enter new answers.
    Each answer are separated by a new line.
    New answers will replace the old ones by the order of which they were specified.
    If more new answers are given, they will be added to the decision.
    If more old answers are given, they will be ignored.
    Remove a Decision Choose a decision.
    Removing a decision will also remove its subsequent answers and decisions
    Remove Answers to a Decision Choose a decision and select multiple answers to be removed by holding Ctrl.
    Removing an answer will also remove its subsequent decisions.
